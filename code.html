Our Story

Built on
craft &
conviction

Maison was born from a simple frustration: a world full of objects that looked beautiful but were built to be replaced. We set out to do the opposite.

We work directly with a small network of artisans across Europe and Japan who share our obsession with material honesty and enduring form. Every piece in our collection has been touched by human hands and tested by time.

What we
stand for

Material Honesty

We use what nature provides — linen, clay, walnut, wool — without masking it. Each material is chosen for its character and its ability to age gracefully.

Slow Making

Nothing in our collection is mass produced. We work with makers who take the time to get it right, even if that means smaller batches and longer waits.

Radical Durability

We design for decades, not seasons. If something breaks, we'll repair it. If it wears, it should wear beautifully. Buy less. Buy better.

Honest Commerce

We publish the cost of everything we make and why we charge what we do. Our artisans are paid fairly. Our prices reflect that.
